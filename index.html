about me

Professionally an Adops specialist working for a global advertising company Teads. In my spare time I create Pinterest content, develop SEO strategies, and explore GEO for my content. I am constantly seeking the balance between technical precision and creative expression.

Underneath all that I am a lover of wisdom – a pseudo philosopher. I believe that every digital footprint we leave behind is a reflection of a deeper intent. My work in AdOps isn't just about pixels and tracking; it's about understanding the human experience in a digital landscape. This dual perspective allows me to approach problems with both a logical mind and a philosophical heart.

Whether I am optimizing a campaign or contemplating the nature of reality, my goal is always the same: to find clarity in the noise and meaning in the data.
